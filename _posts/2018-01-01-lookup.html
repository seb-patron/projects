About this Project

Romanesque. Gothic. Renaissance. Modern. The skys and architecture of Prague are filled with beauty everywhere you look. Every street turn is a new visual delight. It is a shame however, that many of the people of Prague choose to look down at the ground instead. This project is meant to celebrate the beauty of the skys and architecture of Prague, and remind those around to always look up. All photos were taken with a Ricoh GR II and developed in Lightroom.
